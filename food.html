What and where to eat


You will not get hungry in Japan, and that is a promise! They have everything you can think of and even more.
We recommend the combini stores like 7-Eleven, Lawson, or Family Mart for a quick meal on the road. Also, when booking your stay, include the breakfast, if possible. It will save you time in the morning.
When choosing a restaurant, look after the lines. People wait in line almost everywhere. So the bigger the line, the better the food.
We also ventured into smaller, unpopular places. We went into a ramen restaurant in Osaka that was run by this elderly couple, and we ate really well there. The place looked super antique, but the food was soooo good.
